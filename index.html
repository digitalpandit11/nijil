About This Experience

Experience the magic of Paris with our comprehensive full-day city tour, designed to give you the best of the City of Light in just one day. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the world-renowned Louvre Museum, this carefully curated experience covers all the must-see attractions.

Your day begins with a convenient hotel pickup, followed by skip-the-line access to the Eiffel Tower's second floor, where you'll enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Paris. After capturing those perfect photos, indulge in an authentic French lunch at a charming local bistro.

The afternoon continues with a guided tour of the Louvre Museum, where you'll see masterpieces including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. End your perfect day with a relaxing Seine River cruise, gliding past Notre-Dame, Musée d'Orsay, and other iconic landmarks.

Itinerary

9:00 AM
Hotel Pickup
Meet your friendly guide at your hotel lobby. Travel in comfort in our air-conditioned vehicle to the first stop.
10:00 AM
Eiffel Tower Visit (2nd Floor)
Skip the long lines with priority access. Ascend to the second floor for stunning 360° views of Paris. Your guide will share fascinating history and point out landmarks. Photo opportunities included.
12:00 PM
French Gourmet Lunch
Savor a delicious 3-course French meal at an authentic Parisian bistro near the Champs-Élysées. Includes wine pairing and dessert.
2:00 PM
Louvre Museum Guided Tour
Explore the world's largest art museum with skip-the-line entry. See the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, Winged Victory of Samothrace, and more masterpieces with expert commentary.
4:30 PM
Seine River Cruise
Relax on a 1-hour scenic cruise along the Seine. Pass by Notre-Dame Cathedral, Musée d'Orsay, Pont Neuf, and other iconic landmarks while enjoying audio commentary.
6:00 PM
Hotel Drop-off
Return to your hotel with wonderful memories of an unforgettable day in Paris.
